Sentiment Analysis (NLP)

情感分析（自然语言处理）

Definition 情感分析的定义： Opinion mining, analyzes the polarity of documents: + or -

针对文本数据的观点挖掘，文档的情感倾向分析：正 或 反

A popular task in sentiment analysis 情感分析的任务：The classification of documents based on the expressed opinions or emotions of the authors with regard to a particular topic.
针对特定主题的文档，依据作者表达的观点或情感，进行文档分类

The Bag-of-Words Model 词袋模型

将文本数字向量化

Represent text as numerical feature vectors 文本表达为特征向量

情感原因分析

create a vocabulary of unique tokens based on the entire set of documents 基于整个文档集的每个词汇，创建词汇表（索引）

construct a feature vector for each document that contains the counts of how often each unique token occurs in the document 对每一个文档，依据词汇出现频率，构建文档的特征向量

文档的特征向量是稀疏的？一个文档的词汇相对所有文档的词汇表中的词汇量来说是微不足道的

Document文档集:

Vocabulary of unique tokens 词汇表（索引）,一共九个词

Feature vector for each document 文档的特征向量 tf(t, d)

文档的特征向量是词频向量 tf(t, d)，词汇 t 在文档 d 中出现的频率 f

term：词 frequency:p频

n-gram: The contiguous sequences of items 项序列: n-元组
e.g. The sun is shining.
1-gram: “the”, “sun”, “is”, “shining” 1-元组、单元组
2-gram: “the sun”, “sun is”, “is shining” 2-元组
3-gram and 4-gram yield good performances in anti-spam filtering of e-mail messages
3-元组、4-元组对“反垃圾邮件过滤”可获得好的效果

When words occur across multiple documents? 词会在多个文档中出现
Assessing word relevancy 评估“词与文档类别的关联度”

在多个文档频繁出现的词不具备辨识度

Document pre-processing 文档的预处理

Processing documents into tokens 文本拆分为词
Word stemming: transform a word into its root 词干提取

Stop-word Removal 移除停用词

extremely common in all sorts of texts 最常见词 such as: is, and, a, the
no (or only little) useful information that can be used to distinguish between different classes of documents 无用信息

Internet Movie Database IMDb

互联网影评数据集 IMDb

50,000 polar movie reviews: labeled + or -
positive: a movie was rated with more than six stars 多于六星
negative: movie was rated with fewer than five stars 少于五星
Integer class label: 1 = positive and 0 = negative

http://ai.stanford.edu/~amaas/data/sentiment
IMDb preprocessing: imdb_prepro.py

Save as a CSV file: movie_data.csv 保存为CSV文件

Cleaning: unwanted characters 对无用字符做“文本数据清洗”

Logistic Regression Model based on IMDB

基于IMDb的逻辑斯蒂影评文档分类模型

1、当verbose=0时，不输出日志信息 ，诸如进度条、loss、acc这些都不输出；
2、当verbose=1时，输出日志信息，包含诸如进度条、时间、训练集和测试集上的loss、acc；
